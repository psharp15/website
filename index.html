About Me

I'm a physicist with a deep commitment to scientific excellence and community impact. Currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Physics at George Washington University, I combine rigorous experimental research with a passion for education and diversity in STEM.

My work spans experimental nuclear physics, science education, and community engagement. I believe in making physics accessible and inspiring the next generation of scientists.
